Our Story

A family business built on trust & purpose

Credit Control Solutions was founded by Sunil and Deepika after years in the property management industry — where they saw firsthand the toll that credit control disputes took on managers, tenants, and relationships alike.

Originally from Bangalore, India, they moved to New Zealand in 2015 and built this company from the ground up — driven by a shared belief that there is a better, more human way to resolve tenancy disputes.

Inspired by their daughter Tiara, they set out to create a business that operates as a trusted partner to property managers across New Zealand — always professional, always accessible, and always working towards that win-win outcome.
